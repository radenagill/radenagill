Article About SMKN 9 Semarang

Hello, my name is Agil. I attend SMKN 9 Semarang, which I believe is the best school. SMKN 9 Semarang is located in Peterongan, in the middle of the village. It offers several majors including Software Engineering, Accounting, Marketing, and Office Administration.

The school buildings are divided into three parts: the main building, the front building, and the side building. The main building consists of the school gate, security post, teachers' room, marketing lab, accounting lab, business field, and sports building. The front building houses the post office and the Software Engineering lab, while the side building contains the counseling room and the teachers' office.

Life at SMKN 9 Semarang

Every morning, I go to school and am greeted by some teachers and members of the student council. Our class system is similar to university lectures, where we change classrooms for each subject. Our school runs a business center developed by the Marketing department. I usually buy food at the canteen in the main building, and sometimes at the business center next to it.

While our school may have many shortcomings such as cramped buildings, slow Wi-Fi, and inadequate facilities, it always strives to improve. Efforts include providing free Wi-Fi in all rooms and improving other school facilities. Our school also regularly organizes social activities such as environmental care through paper recycling and, most recently, a blood donation drive in collaboration with the Semarang Red Cross.

Spirit of SMKN 9 Semarang

Although our school may not be as large or as popular as others in the city, we have a strong spirit and pride in being part of the SMKN 9 Semarang community. Here, we learn not only to acquire knowledge but also to become empathetic, broad-minded individuals ready to face the challenges of the world.
